Magzter is the world's largest and fastest growing digital newsstand. Headquartered in New York, Magzter is registered as a Delaware C Corp. The Magzter app was launched in June 2011 and is available across multiple devices. Magzter stands out from other newsstands as it presents a unique revenue-share based business model to publishers, making it easy for them to distribute digital editions of their magazines and newspapers globally with NO upfront cost. This has resulted in over 4,000 leading publishers uploading thousands of magazines and newspapers on Magzter.

Magzter is the only self-service newsstand that offers publishers a host of special tools that simplifies the digital publishing process; along with its proprietary Orey Click Publishing® System, it has facilitated the move from print to digital for publication houses the world over through a one touch process. Magzter also creates individual branded apps for publishers and service providers enabling them to stay connected with their target audiences at all times. Pushing boundaries with technology and staying true to the cause of digital publishing; Magzter gives its users a world-class reading experience.

Here, readers can browse; preview and purchase magazines and newspapers of their choice with a single touch and read it on upto 5 of their chosen smart devices. That's not all; when publishers embed bonus video and audio content in their digital editions, Magzter brings it to you—right there! Flip through the pages, click on links in articles and move right into HD video at its best. Enter a contest, take a poll, or follow an interesting website, all without having to leave the issue which underlines its user friendliness.
